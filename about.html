About Us

Raperty is a modern property business that answers your every need in property and has been established since 2011.

Raperty has been present in Batam, Medan, Palembang, Jakarta, Bogor, Depok, Tangerang, Bekasi, Bandung, Yogyakarta, Semarang, Solo, Surabaya, Sidoarjo, Malang, Jember, Bali, Makassar, Balikpapan, Samarinda.
